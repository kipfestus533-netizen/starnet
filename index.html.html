About StarNet
🛰 Satellite Broadband Provider

Who We Are

StarNet is a leading satellite internet reseller powered by the Starlink low-Earth orbit constellation. We connect homes, businesses, and remote communities across Africa and beyond with high-speed, low-latency broadband from space.

Our Mission

We believe fast, reliable internet is a right — not a privilege. Our mission is to make satellite connectivity accessible and affordable for everyone, from urban professionals to rural enterprises.

Why StarNet?

  • Instant activation — no technician visits required
  • Flexible monthly bundles with no long-term contracts
  • Secure payments via Kora Pay — M-Pesa, card, and bank transfer
  • 24/7 support via Telegram and email
  • Coverage in 180+ countries

Our Network

Powered by the Starlink satellite constellation operating at ~550 km altitude, StarNet delivers average speeds of 220+ Mbps with latency as low as 40ms — a game-changer for remote connectivity.

StarNet is an independent reseller and is not affiliated with or endorsed by SpaceX or Starlink.
